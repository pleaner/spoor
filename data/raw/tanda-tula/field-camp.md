## Positioning

The "adventurous little sibling" of Tanda Tula Safari Camp — an authentic, low-luxury walking safari camp for travellers "happy to go without luxury and immerse themselves in nature." Founding principle: "Treading softly on the surface of the earth." A mobile/temporary tented eco-camp focused on guided **walking safaris** led by "some of the best trackers in the industry." Owned and run by Don and Nina Scott.

## Other sources

### True Travel (https://true.travel/africa/south-africa/accommodation/tanda-tula-field-camp/)
- **Positioning/philosophy:** "Treading softly on the surface of the earth"; low-impact safari experiences with a commitment to walking safaris.
- **Capacity:** "four explorer tents," accommodating a limited number of guests for exclusive-use experiences (max 8 guests — see WebSearch below).
- **Tent & bathroom:** tents styled "in the style of a bygone era"; each has its own outdoor bathroom equipped with hot water bucket showers, eco toilets, and amenities.
- **Location:** Timbavati Private Nature Reserve, South Africa.
- **Activities:** primary focus is walking safaris "with some of the best trackers in the industry"; walks emphasize seeking Big Five game with guides teaching about nature and wildlife. Game drives also available, arranged flexibly.
- **Operational model:** "set up and taken down for each exclusive use safari," with locations varying seasonally based on game activity. Staff includes a private guide, chef, camp host, and dedicated camp personnel.
- **Rates / child policy / operating months:** not specified on this page.

### WebSearch corroboration (Go2Africa, Yellow Zebra, Siyabona, k2c, The Luxury Safari Company)
- "Set within a private concession in the Kruger National Park's Timbavati Private Nature Reserve, this mobile tented eco-camp focuses on guided walking safaris and accommodates **up to eight guests**. Each tent has its own outdoor bathroom with hot water bucket shower, eco loo and amenities."
- Exclusive-use basis; "Between March and October Tanda Tula offers an option to camp out in the bush on an exclusive use basis, with Tanda Tula's field camp accommodating a maximum of eight guests." Located in the **eastern region of the Timbavati Private Nature Reserve**, bordering Kruger, in an exclusive concession of **6,500 hectares**, offering guided walking safaris led by "iconic and highly trained trackers and guides."
- Siyabona lists it under **Klaserie Private Nature Reserve**, Greater Kruger.
- The Luxury Safari Company ("All Change at Tanda Tula"): "Tanda Tula Field Camp... is owned and run by Don and Nina Scott and is described as the adventurous little sibling of Tanda Tula Safari Camp, a permanent property in the Timbavati's eastern region. This is an authentic walking camp perfect for travelers happy to go without luxury and immerse themselves in nature, with a focus on walks." It notes Field Camp "operates within the Klaserie Private Game Reserve from **April 1 to October 30**, and is set up and taken down for each exclusive safari booking, with locations changing throughout the year."
