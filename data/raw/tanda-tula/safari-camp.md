## Positioning

The flagship, owner-run luxury camp of the Tanda Tula group. "Your camp in Africa" with "the warm of African hospitality in a home you've always dreamed of." Tagline-level positioning: "a safari camp should be more than just beautiful — it should immerse you in nature and feed your senses." Marketed as "an unhurried, soulful African safari" — family-owned, Big Five territory, in the Timbavati Private Nature Reserve bordering Kruger National Park.

Completely redesigned by architect Nick Plewman and reimagined with modern African design and a "lighter footprint"; the all-new Safari Camp reopened in June (after a full rebuild during 2023). Set overlooking the Nhlaralumi (Nlharalumi) riverbed, with canvas-and-glass interiors. Interiors "pay tribute to South Africa's Tsonga people, the original custodians of this land, blending cultural heritage with modern luxuries" — "deep aubergine, textured wood, and intricate patterns of Tsonga tradition."

### Transfers
- **Road:** air-conditioned vehicle from Hoedspruit or Kruger Mpumalanga International Airport. Timbavati entrance fee R 360/vehicle (self-drive only).

Only FedAir permitted at Rockfig; other charter companies must request special permission through Tanda Tula Reservations (approval rare). Alternatives: Hoedspruit Civil or Eastgate airports with road transfers (additional fees apply).
