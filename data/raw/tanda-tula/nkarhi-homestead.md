## Positioning

Tanda Tula's exclusive-use, sole-use "home from home" in the Timbavati — a self-catering / fully-catered private villa (also spelled "Nkhari"). "Nkarhi" means **'time'** in the Tsonga language; "a place that inspires relaxation and allows connections to be created and cherished." Described as "one of the oldest homesteads in the country." Farm-style family home, off-grid and solar-powered, elevated above plains and waterways. "Off-grid with total exclusivity."
