## Review 3
**Reviewer:** Martin Fey
**Date:** October 2019
**Rating:** 5/5
**Title:** Safari at Tswalu Kalahari Game Reserve
**Text:** Recommends minimum two full days, preferably four. Notes unique wildlife sightings unavailable elsewhere, including pangolins and aardvarks. Cautions that animal density is lower than Kruger or Serengeti. Recommends "for connoisseurs rather not for safari beginners."
