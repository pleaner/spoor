## Positioning
Tree Camp is positioned as the most stylish, design-led and adults-oriented of Londolozi's camps — "the most stylish family run camp in Africa" (Wetu) and "a sanctuary of elegance and simplicity" (website). A small (6-suite) Relais & Châteaux camp themed as "a celebration of Leopards, Leadwoods and Lanterns." Positioning line: Tree Camp "embodies the very essence of Londolozi's continuous striving to transcend and redefine the journey to the wilderness." Adults-focused: no children under 16 unless the camp is booked exclusively.
