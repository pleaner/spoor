## Positioning
"The heart and soul of Londolozi." Varty Camp is the original, founding camp of the group, emphasising family and heritage. The campfire has burned at the camp for over 90 years (website) / over 86 years (Wetu), making it "the symbolic heart and soul of Londolozi." Described as having "a pleasingly down-to-earth quality" where "children always have a place." The original Sparta Camp (1926), where Londolozi began, sits within Varty Camp.

## Website
<!-- collected: 2026-06-22; cadence: 30d -->
Source: https://www.londolozi.com/en/accommodation/varty-camp

- Tagline: "The heart and soul of Londolozi"
- "This original camp is a tapestry of brick paths, dolerite rock walls, waterfalls and tangled vines echoing simple spaces with unencumbered river view backdrops."
- Heritage: "the campfire has burned at Varty Camp for over 90 years."
- Accommodation: 10 Chalets with interconnecting room options. Each chalet features "its own private swimming pool, outdoor shower and outdoor dining sala, framed by timber decks raised off the ground under the shade of an ancient riverine forest."
- Rate: "From R27,950 pp per night."
- Guest policy: children over age 6 welcome; children ages 6–16 accommodated at Varty and Founders camps; children 6+ permitted on game drives.
- Facilities (access to): Guest Gym, Photographic Studio, Living Boutique, Healing House (massage and specialized wellness treatments), Cubs Den, Yoga Deck (located at Founders Camp). The original "Sparta Camp" represents "where Londolozi began in 1926."
- Activities: between-drive options include bush walks, village experiences, massage, yoga, and boutique shopping. Game drives led by professional rangers with "350 years of experience between them."
