## Positioning
"Pioneer Camp is a symphony of style, bushveld architecture and nature" (Dave Varty) — "the culmination of five decades of design experience in the safari industry" and "the most intimate family-run safari homestead in Southern Africa." The most secluded of Londolozi's camps: three private river-view suites perfect for couples/honeymoons, yet adaptable into a private bush homestead for multi-generational family groups of 6–12 people. Wetu tagline: "A Tribute to Authentic Conservation Safari Pioneer."
