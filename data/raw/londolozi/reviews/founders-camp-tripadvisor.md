## AGthing56 — May 2025 — 5/5

**Title:** (no title)

> "My husband and I traveled to Southern Africa for my birthday; a dream vacation that was so worth the wait. Our stay at Londolozi Founders Camp is one of my top travel experiences, a memorable stay that will be forever etched in my soul. The overall product that Londolozi provides is unparalleled. How could you not be happy here? Every single staff member is so kind, welcoming, and professional. From the very start, I worked with Emma to book our stay directly, as we did not use a travel agent. She insured everything we needed was taken care of, including documenting my gluten allergy and the fact this trip was for a milestone b-day. Upon arrival, you are greeted with the managers and staff that are dedicated to Founders Camp. Hannah and Chloe were exceptional managers of Founders. They cater to your every need, ensure your day is managed, and are there to address any questions. They are genuinely interested in hearing about your experience, guiding you to breakfast, lunch, or any activity on the premises. I was blown away, when they greeted me to an entire birthday cake one evening for dinner – 100% gluten free. Speaking of the food – you will be spoiled! You will never be hungry, and the food is exceptional, fresh, and made with love. Not to mention each day the menu is different for lunch and dinner. I have to call out the care they give to your allergies. I've traveled around the world and never have I been fed so well as a gluten free person. We also loved our butler Thoko. She's an absolute gem. The star of our stay was of course the game drives. We had the 'A' team with Veatence Ubisi 'V' as our Tracker, and Megan Wade 'Meg' as our Ranger. The two were a perfect pair and after 6 drives, we were legitimately sad to leave what felt like new family members. The fun part of going on a drive, is that they are very educational. It's cool watching them track animal paw prints, etc., to help find the animals you're looking for. On our first drive in the afternoon, we spotted 2 leopards, including a cub that was 3 years old. Later on in the drive we spotted another leopard. We saw all kinds of birds, lions on 3 separate occasions, along with baby cubs. We were lucky to come across multiple herds of elephants with their babies! On our last morning, we still hadn't seen Rhinos. V and Meg worked hard and within the first hour we spotted not 1 but 5 Rhinos. They wanted to make sure we saw them before leaving. Thank you Meg and V! I have to also shout out Meg for helping to edit a few photos using Lightroom in their photography studio. I bought a camera specifically for this trip and learned how to turn it on a month before arriving. I managed to capture some great shots, which Meg helped me to enhance. I purchased a print so I can forever remember this entirely beautify experience at Londolozi. Thank you for making the best birthday ever! We will certainly be back."
