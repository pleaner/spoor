## Positioning
"Honoring the Past, Celebrating the Present." Founders Camp honours the early Londolozi founders and alumni whose efforts "distilled the legacy of the past 50 years into the creation of the essence of safari spirit." A "Londolozi signature camp" where "classic meets modern" — "clean and uncomplicated and really comfortable," offering "all the comforts of a stylish African home" with "an insider's view of the secrets of river life." Family-friendly and modern; home of the group yoga deck.
