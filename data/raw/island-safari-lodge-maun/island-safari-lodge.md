## Positioning

Established 1973. A 300-acre private island reserve 10 km from Maun Airport on the banks of the Thamalakane River — positioned as a value-for-money gateway and transit lodge for the Okavango Delta. Caters to independent travellers, overlanders, family groups, and self-drive campers as well as guests booking day trips and multi-day mobile safaris into Moremi and the Delta. The island reserve functions as a 113Ha registered mini Game Park, offering self-guided and guided trails on-property in addition to outbound excursions. Operating name: Island Safari Lodge & Island Mobile Safaris; managed through African Secrets Travel & Tours.

- **TripAdvisor collection stopped at or30** (30 reviews captured from 367 total). Older reviews not collected.
- **Single property.** Wetu and all third-party sources confirm one bookable property under this lodge group. Room categories (Heritage Chalet, Heritage Room, Heritage Family Room, Camp Site) are all sub-types of the single Island Safari Lodge property, not separate bookable camps.
