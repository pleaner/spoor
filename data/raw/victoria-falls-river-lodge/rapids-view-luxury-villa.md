## Positioning
The second of the group's two **exclusive private villas**, on the banks of the Zambezi within the Zambezi National Park, overlooking the river. The more family-oriented of the two villas: configured as **2 Family suites** sleeping up to four adults and four-to-six children, with flexible bedding (King/twin plus murphy-style and bunk beds). Personal butler, private chef's kitchen, private lounge/dining, private airport transfers. Bookable standard per-person or sole-use ("private activities upon request").
