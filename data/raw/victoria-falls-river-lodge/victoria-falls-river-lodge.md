## Positioning
The flagship riverside camp of the group: a 5-star tented lodge on the banks of the Zambezi River, inside the Zambezi National Park, ~8 mi (≈13 km) upstream of Victoria Falls. This is the main, full-service property containing the communal hub (lobby, restaurant, bar/fire pit, pool, spa, fitness centre, activity/kiddies desk) that the satellite camps connect to. Accommodation is "luxury canvas-under-thatch structures." It is family-capable (the only part of the group with dedicated family tents and the "Little Livingstone's" children's programme) — distinct from the adults-only Malachite (couples) and Island Treehouse (island/butler) products and the exclusive villas. Wetu special interests: Adventure, Birding, Fishing, Gourmet, Hiking, Indigenous Culture/Art, Leisure, Nature, Relaxation, Star Gazing, Wildlife.
