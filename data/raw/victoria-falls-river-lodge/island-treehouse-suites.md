## Positioning
The group's most exclusive island product: butler-serviced luxury suites on **private Kandahar Island**, accessed exclusively by boat from the mainland. Elevated on timber decks over the river and jungle, connected by raised walkways. Adults/teens only (**minimum age 12**). The **Starbed Treehouse** is a premium double-storey variant within this product (not a separate camp): a rooftop open-air "Starbed" for sleeping under the stars, telescope deck, and a complimentary spa voucher. Highest per-person rates in the group after sole-use villas. Includes superior beverages and laundry (a step up from the main tents).

**Group T&Cs (Appendix A) applicable to Island Treehouse** (full text in main-lodge dossier):
- FIT = 1–6 paying guests/night; Group = 7+.
- FIT cancellation: 60+ days free; 59–30 = 50%; 29–14 = 75%; 13–0 = 100%.
- Transfers: direct + shared from/to Victoria Falls Airport (then island boat).
- **Children policy (Island Treehouse & Malachite):** no persons younger than 12; each suite max 2 sharing guests; child in own suite charged full adult rate; plunge pools not cordoned/lifeguarded.
- **Tour Leaders/Guides/Pilots:** owing to limited Island suites, NO reduced rate; no guide accommodation on Kandahar Island — a full-rate Island suite must be booked, or the guide may use tented guide accommodation at the riverside lodge.
- Group concession (Island Treehouse): every 9 paying guests = 1 free (10th free), min 2-night stay.
- Tourism levy (2%) and 15% VAT included.
