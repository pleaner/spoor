## Positioning
A dedicated, intimate **couples' retreat** set apart from the main lodge — "the perfect Luxury Retreat for Couples Seeking the Ultimate African Experience." Located **one mile (≈1.6 km) downstream** of the main Victoria Falls River Lodge along the Zambezi, within the Zambezi National Park. Strictly adults/teens: **minimum age 12 years**. Smaller and more secluded than the main camp, connected by raised walkways to its own communal facilities; accessed via boat transfer from the main lodge / its own Malachite jetty. Frequently recommended in guest reviews as a honeymoon address.
