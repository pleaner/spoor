## Positioning
One of two **exclusive private villas** in the group, on the banks of the Zambezi within the Zambezi National Park, with "Uninterrupted views of the Zambezi River." Designed for private/family groups, with personal butler, private chef's kitchen, and private communal lounge/dining — bookable on a standard per-person basis or for **sole use** of the whole villa. Private (not shared) airport transfers and private game activities (sole-use). Distinct from Rapids View by its layout: 2 King/Twin suites + 1 Family suite (sleeps up to ~6 adults + 2 children).
