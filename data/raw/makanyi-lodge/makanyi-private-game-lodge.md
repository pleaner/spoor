## Positioning
An intimate eight-suite luxury safari lodge in the Timbavati Nature Reserve, adjoining Kruger National Park in South Africa's southern sector. Operates only three safari vehicles across 8,000 hectares — positioning is strongly around uncrowded, private wildlife immersion rather than large-volume tourism. Family-owned since 2015, ranked #1 Resort in South Africa and #2 Resort in the World by Condé Nast Traveler Readers' Choice Awards 2024 (USA & UK editions); ranked #4 in South Africa in 2025. "Warm, homely atmosphere" over corporate scale. Targets honeymooners, couples, wildlife photographers, conservation-minded travellers.
