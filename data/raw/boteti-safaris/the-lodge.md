## Positioning

The Lodge is the flagship accommodation of Boteti Safaris — a small, intimate riverside tented camp on the banks of the Boteti River in the Xobe District, near Maun, Botswana. It positions itself as a personalised full-board hub for exploring southern Botswana's major game reserves (Okavango Delta, Moremi, Khwai, Savuti, Chobe, Makgadikgadi Pans) without requiring multiple camp relocations. The property leans on its riverside setting, skilled local guides, and "authentic" locally-owned identity rather than ultra-luxury credentials. It is gated, but sits adjacent to a local community, so livestock (donkeys, cows, goats) and domestic sounds are occasionally encountered — a recurring theme in guest reviews.
