## Positioning

Camissa House is a 5-star luxury boutique hotel in Oranjezicht, Cape Town — one of only 8 rooms perched on the slopes of Table Mountain at 28 Bridle Road, with the mountain immediately behind and uninterrupted views over the city and Atlantic Ocean. Its core differentiation is extreme intimacy (maximum 16 guests), all-inclusive pricing (house beverages, meals, dinner transfers), 24-hour butler service, and a design inspired by the Cape Floral Kingdom. It is the sister property to the award-winning MannaBay Hotel and is linked via a joint "Cape Town and Sabi Sand" promotion with Silvan Safari Lodge in the Sabi Sand. Positioned as the ideal city bookend to a safari itinerary.
