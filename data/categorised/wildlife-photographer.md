## Makanyi Private Game Lodge
Makanyi is the only property whose evaluation explicitly courts photographers: it positions toward "honeymooners, couples, wildlife photographers" and offers a private vehicle and a professional photographic tutor. Its low-density model — only three safari vehicles across 8,000 hectares, delivering "uncrowded, high-touch game viewing" — gives a photographer the unhurried, exclusive vehicle access and clean sightlines that serious wildlife photography depends on.
