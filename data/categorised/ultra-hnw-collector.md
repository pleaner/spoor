## Londolozi Pioneer Camp
Pioneer offers "top-end experiential and personal luxury" in a Relais & Châteaux setting, sold as the most secluded camp or as an entire private homestead with a notably rich infrastructure — explorer lounge, interactive Homestead Kitchen, lookout deck and full-length lap pool. The whole-camp exclusive-use option, capped at six adults, is precisely the kind of buy-the-entire-thing privacy a collector of rare luxury experiences seeks. [source](../evaluated/londolozi/pioneer-camp.md)

## Londolozi Tree Camp
The evaluation describes a "high-end" design-led product — Ralph Lauren wallpaper, chocolate plaited leather beds, contemplation salas and private plunge pools, all under Relais & Châteaux membership. Reviewers call it "the definition of living up to the hype" and "the most extraordinary travel experience I've ever had," marking it as a trophy-grade, design-forward camp for a discerning ultra-luxury guest. [source](../evaluated/londolozi/tree-camp.md)

## Londolozi Private Granite Suites
Granite is "the most expensive and most exclusive of the five Londolozi camps," built for "the discerning traveller who appreciates the finer details": three standalone suites for the exclusive use of six guests, a distinctive silver-and-charcoal palette, a sunset river bar and sunken cocktail lounge suspended above the Sand River, and Relais & Châteaux status. A reviewer rates it "probably the best game lodge in South Africa," underscoring its appeal at the very top of the collector tier. [source](../evaluated/londolozi/private-granite-suites.md)
