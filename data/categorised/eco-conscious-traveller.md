## Makanyi Private Game Lodge
Makanyi runs a deliberately low-impact operation — "only three safari vehicles across 8,000 hectares" — and levies a mandatory R620 per-person conservation contribution that is never discounted, even under specials. The combination of low vehicle density and a ring-fenced conservation levy gives an eco-conscious guest evidence that the property limits its footprint and directs spend to conservation. [source](../evaluated/makanyi-lodge/makanyi-private-game-lodge.md)

## Tanda Tula Safari Camp
The evaluation records an age-banded "sustainability levy" (R1,815 per person for 12+, R910 for 6–11) itemised on every rate and never discounted, on a camp set across 55,626 hectares of protected traversing. The explicit sustainability charge and the scale of the protected wilderness are concrete signals for an eco-minded traveller. [source](../evaluated/tanda-tula/safari-camp.md)

## Londolozi Varty Camp
Varty's rate carries a mandatory Guest Conservation Contribution (R573.99 per person, the lower Founders-and-Varty band), so every booking channels a fixed levy into conservation. For an eco-conscious guest, that built-in contribution evidences how guest spending supports the reserve's conservation work. [source](../evaluated/londolozi/varty-camp.md)

## Londolozi Founders Camp
Founders applies the same Guest Conservation Contribution (R573.99 per person, lower band) as a separate, undiscounted line on every stay. The mandatory conservation levy is the camp's clearest eco signal: a guaranteed portion of each booking is directed to conservation. [source](../evaluated/londolozi/founders-camp.md)

## Londolozi Pioneer Camp
Pioneer carries the higher "Tree, Granite and Pioneer" band Guest Conservation Contribution (R957.50 per person), itemised separately on every rate. The mandatory, ring-fenced conservation levy gives an eco-conscious traveller assurance that their spend contributes directly to conservation. [source](../evaluated/londolozi/pioneer-camp.md)

## Londolozi Tree Camp
Tree Camp's evaluation lists a mandatory Guest Conservation Contribution (R957.50 per person, higher band) as the only line added on top of the all-inclusive rate. That dedicated, non-negotiable conservation charge is its central eco-conscious credential. [source](../evaluated/londolozi/tree-camp.md)

## Londolozi Private Granite Suites
Granite applies the same higher-band Guest Conservation Contribution (R957.50 per person) on its couple-only rate. The mandatory conservation levy is the evaluated evidence that each stay channels guest spending into conservation, the core concern of an eco-conscious traveller. [source](../evaluated/londolozi/private-granite-suites.md)
