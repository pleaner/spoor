## Makanyi Private Game Lodge
Makanyi's evaluation puts couples and honeymooners first: eight suites, a minimum child age of 12, and an explicit Honeymoon special (50% off on proof of marriage), with the property positioned toward "honeymooners, couples, wildlife photographers." The experience it sells — uncrowded, high-touch game viewing on only three vehicles across 8,000 hectares, with Pool Suites adding a private plunge pool — is well matched to a couple seeking seclusion and romance over a graded room ladder. [source](../evaluated/makanyi-lodge/makanyi-private-game-lodge.md)

## Tanda Tula Safari Camp
The evaluation notes couples are well served in the Safari Suite and that the camp carries a dedicated honeymoon special, making it a genuine fit for a couple's special-occasion stay. The all-inclusive single-product format (all meals, drinks, two activities) and fly-in orientation on 55,626 hectares of traversing support an unhurried, romantic booking, though the 2-night minimum makes it a considered rather than casual escape. [source](../evaluated/tanda-tula/safari-camp.md)

## Londolozi Pioneer Camp
Pioneer is described as the "honeymoon-or-whole-family" camp and, per-suite, "the most secluded of Londolozi's camps … perfect for honeymoons and private getaways" — three deeply private river-view suites with in-suite plunge pools and Relais & Châteaux affiliation. The Wetu copy frames it as "an ideal romantic setting," and a reviewer recalls it as the "Ultimate luxury safari experience" on a honeymoon, making seclusion and romance its core appeal for couples. [source](../evaluated/londolozi/pioneer-camp.md)

## Londolozi Tree Camp
The evaluation calls Tree Camp "squarely an adults' couples-and-honeymoon camp," enforced by a no-under-16 policy that keeps it child-free for non-exclusive bookings. Its design-led suites — private plunge pools, contemplation salas, the image of "champagne in hand as elephants pass in the river below" — and a "Magical Honeymoon" among its reviews mark it as a purpose-built romantic retreat for couples. [source](../evaluated/londolozi/tree-camp.md)

## Londolozi Private Granite Suites
Granite is the "adults-only, special-occasion couples camp" at the top of the Londolozi ladder, explicitly "ideal for honeymooning couples or small groups celebrating special occasions" and barred to children under 16. With only three standalone suites for the exclusive use of six guests, each with a private heated pool and riverview bath, it trades flexibility for the privacy and design-forward intimacy a honeymooning couple seeks. [source](../evaluated/londolozi/private-granite-suites.md)
