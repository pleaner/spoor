## Londolozi Pioneer Camp
The evaluation flags Pioneer among Londolozi's "most design-forward suites" and lists a string of visually distinctive features — a sky walkway linking the suites, a Pioneer lookout deck and breakfast balcony, a full-length lap pool and in-suite plunge pools. That photogenic, design-rich homestead gives a content creator a steady supply of striking, shareable settings. [source](../evaluated/londolozi/pioneer-camp.md)

## Londolozi Tree Camp
Tree Camp is explicitly one of the camps targeting the "most design-forward" end, with Ralph Lauren wallpaper, chocolate plaited leather beds, contemplation salas and private plunge pools, plus the signature scene of "champagne in hand as elephants pass in the river below." Its highly styled, instantly recognisable aesthetic is ideal for visually driven social content. [source](../evaluated/londolozi/tree-camp.md)

## Londolozi Private Granite Suites
Granite is the most design-forward camp in the group, with a distinctive silver, charcoal and elephant-grey palette, an outside riverview bath, private heated pools and a "sunset river bar and sunken cocktail lounge … suspended high above the Sand River." Those bold, photogenic spaces make it a natural backdrop for an influencer's feed. [source](../evaluated/londolozi/private-granite-suites.md)
