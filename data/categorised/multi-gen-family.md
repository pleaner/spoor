## Tanda Tula Safari Camp
The evaluation finds Tanda Tula "built for families and multi-generational groups more than any other persona" — one of the few cards with a dedicated two-bedroom Family Suite and a minimum child age of 6 with a real child rate. The benchmark family is feasible in all 12 months, and the mix of seven Safari Suites and two Family Suites comfortably absorbs a multi-generational party. [source](../evaluated/tanda-tula/safari-camp.md)

## Londolozi Varty Camp
Varty is the founding family-and-heritage camp where "the emphasis on family heritage means that children always have a place," welcoming children from age 6, and with ten chalets it has the most capacity of any single Londolozi camp. The evaluation names it the best all-rounder and natural choice for families and larger parties on price, with the Cubs Den and private-pool chalets reinforcing its multi-generational suitability. [source](../evaluated/londolozi/varty-camp.md)

## Londolozi Founders Camp
Founders is the "family flagship" of Londolozi — one of only three camps accepting children (from age 6) and uniquely fitted with purpose-built family rooms: three Family Chalets, a two-bedroom Superior Family Chalet for "families of 4 with young children or teenagers," and inter-leading Superior Chalets that "work incredibly well for families of 4 with older children." It is positioned for "modern-day families" and sleeps 20 across ten rooms, making it a strong multi-generational base. [source](../evaluated/londolozi/founders-camp.md)

## Londolozi Pioneer Camp
Pioneer's defining mode for this archetype is the whole-camp buyout: a "private bush homestead for family groups of 6-12 people where a sky walkway links interconnected luxury family suites," with children welcome from age 6 so the family persona is feasible. The evaluation flags its sweet spot as "a multi-generational family or small group of up to twelve wanting exclusive use of an entire luxury homestead," backed by an interactive Homestead Kitchen, lookout deck and lap pool. [source](../evaluated/londolozi/pioneer-camp.md)
