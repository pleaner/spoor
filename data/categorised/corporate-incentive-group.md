## Makanyi Private Game Lodge
The evaluation finds a group of eight adults "well served" — eight suites priced across four Luxury Suites — and notes that group and exclusive-use terms exist in the rate card, so the whole lodge can be taken for an incentive party. The all-inclusive, high-touch product on a private, uncrowded reserve makes it a strong, comparatively affordable buyout for a corporate group. [source](../evaluated/makanyi-lodge/makanyi-private-game-lodge.md)

## Tanda Tula Safari Camp
Tanda Tula's evaluation confirms groups of up to eight "fit comfortably across the 7 Safari + 2 Family Suites," with a single fully-inclusive product and complimentary airstrip transfers that simplify a group itinerary. Its capacity and one-rate-covers-everything structure suit a corporate incentive party wanting a turnkey, exclusive-feeling camp. [source](../evaluated/tanda-tula/safari-camp.md)

## Londolozi Varty Camp
Varty has "the most capacity of any single Londolozi camp" — ten chalets — and the evaluation explicitly lists "larger adult groups" among the parties it suits, seating eight adults across four Standard Chalets at the cheapest benchmark group figure of any Londolozi camp. That combination of capacity and value makes it a practical base for an incentive group wanting the full Londolozi experience. [source](../evaluated/londolozi/varty-camp.md)

## Londolozi Founders Camp
Founders sleeps 20 across ten rooms and the evaluation names "adult groups of up to eight (or more)" among its core fits, with a mix of room types that lets a group be seated economically. Its relaxed, modern aesthetic and group-wide inclusions make it a comfortable, well-sized camp for a corporate incentive party. [source](../evaluated/londolozi/founders-camp.md)

## Londolozi Tree Camp
The evaluation finds the eight-adult group feasible across four of Tree Camp's six suites and observes that "a small multi-room buyout works well for a friends' group or a family of adult children." For an adults-only incentive group seeking a design-led, Relais & Châteaux camp with no children present, the near-buyout of this six-suite camp is a fitting, high-end option. [source](../evaluated/londolozi/tree-camp.md)
